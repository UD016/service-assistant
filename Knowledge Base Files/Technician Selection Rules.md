# Technician Selection Rules

## Purpose

This document provides guidance for selecting the most appropriate technician based on territory, technical expertise, certifications, customer requirements, and operational considerations.

These rules are intended as guidance and do not replace coordinator judgment, technician availability, scheduling constraints, or customer commitments.

# General Selection Hierarchy

When multiple technicians are qualified, prioritize:

1. Safety requirements
2. Security clearance requirements
3. Technical expertise
4. Territory proximity
5. Customer familiarity
6. Availability
7. Workload balance
8. Technician preference

When in doubt, assign the closest qualified technician who can safely and effectively perform the work.
